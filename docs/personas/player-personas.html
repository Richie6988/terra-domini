🤖 AGENT COUNCIL — DESIGN CRITIQUE

6 specialist agents analyze the 10 personas and mandate the 28 features to build.
UX AGENT · Onboarding Specialist
🎯 Critical Finding: 60-Second Rule
Yasmine and Fatou will abandon before minute 2. The current codebase has ZERO onboarding. This is the single highest-churn risk. Mandate: Interactive 5-step tutorial, GPS-centered map on first load, first territory claimed within 60 seconds of opening the app. No exceptions.
SOCIAL AGENT · Retention Specialist
👥 Critical Finding: Social Vacuum
No friend system. No player profiles. No share mechanism. No referral program. Zhang Wei and Priya are social multipliers — each represents 50+ players. Without social features, they bring nobody. Mandate: Friend system, invite links, share cards, creator affiliate program. These 4 features unlock 10× distribution.
MONETIZATION AGENT · ARPU Specialist
💰 Critical Finding: Tariq Is Untapped
High rollers like Tariq (Dubai) are capped at €99.99 purchase and get zero prestige signals. This is leaving 10× ARPU on the table. One Tariq = 100 Yasmines in revenue. Mandate: €499/€999 whale bundles, VIP tier system (Gold/Platinum/Diamond), public territory leaderboard, landmark portfolio dashboard.
LOCALIZATION AGENT · Global Markets
🌍 Critical Finding: 4 Languages Blocking 60% TAM
French (Yasmine, Fatou), Portuguese (Sofia), Japanese (Kenji), Mandarin (Zhang Wei), Arabic (Tariq), Hindi (Priya) — these 6 languages represent the majority of our target markets. Currently EN-only. Mandate: i18n framework with auto-detect. Priority order: FR → PT-BR → JA → ZH-CN → AR → HI.
TRUST AGENT · Web3 Legitimacy
🔐 Critical Finding: Magnus Will Kill Launch
If Magnus (blockchain dev) publicly calls TDC a scam coin, we lose the entire Web3 community. He WILL audit the contract. His findings WILL be tweeted. Mandate: Contract audit (CertiK), timelocked multisig treasury, published tokenomics whitepaper, Polygonscan verification. Must be done before any public announcement.
ACCESSIBILITY AGENT · Global Equity
📱 Critical Finding: Amara Represents 2B Players
Low-bandwidth players on 2G/3G (Nigeria, Senegal, rural India) cannot play at all if maps require 4G. The WebSocket approach also breaks on poor connections. Mandate: Progressive tile loading, HTTP polling fallback, offline resource accumulation with sync, integrated custodial wallet (no MetaMask required), under-100KB initial load.
