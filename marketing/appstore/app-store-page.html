WHAT IS TERRA DOMINI?

The first strategy game built on real Earth. Every hexagon is a real location — from your street corner to the Eiffel Tower. Conquer territories, form alliances, fight for Control Towers. And earn real TerraDominiCoin (TDC) from brand advertising on your territories.

🗺️
Real Satellite Maps
4.2 billion playable hexagons derived from OpenStreetMap. Real terrain affects combat — mountains defend, coasts unlock naval, urban zones earn credits.
⚔️
Grand Strategy Combat
Pure strategy, no twitch skills. 5 unit types, 5 defense tiers. Surprise attacks, sieges, alliance raids. Battle timers from 4h to 72h based on territory size.
🪙
Earn Real TDC
TerraDominiCoin (ERC-20, Polygon). Earn from brand ads on your territories. Trade on QuickSwap. Withdraw to your wallet. 70% of ad revenue goes to players.
📡
Live World Events
Real geopolitical events trigger in-game effects. Strait of Hormuz blockade? Energy production drops in Gulf territories. The world is your game board.
🤝
Alliance Diplomacy
Form Squads (5), Guilds (25), or Federations (500+). Shared treasury, diplomatic treaties, geographic peace lines. Real geopolitics in miniature.
🗼
Control Tower Wars
500 global strategic chokepoints — ports, mountain passes, capitals. 3 capture events daily. 2-hour windows. Your alliance vs the world.
