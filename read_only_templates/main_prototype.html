Universe Lore

The Awakening

In 2042, a global energy crisis fractures civilization. Nations collapse. In their place, commanders — ordinary people wielding a new technology called HEXOD — begin claiming hexagonal territories across the planet. Each hex is tied to a real-world location, a piece of Earth itself.

Crystals & Power

Beneath certain hexes lie Crystal deposits — the universal currency of the new world. Commanders mine, trade, and fight for these resources. Alliances form and break. Empires rise and fall.

The Hunt

Legend speaks of ancient tokens — cards imbued with the essence of real-world events. When earthquakes shake, volcanoes erupt, or storms rage, tokens materialize at exact coordinates. Only the bravest commanders can claim them.

Game Mechanics

Territory System

The world map is divided into hexagonal cells. Mint adjacent hexes to expand. Distant minting requires special items from the Shop. Group hexes into Kingdoms for bonus extraction rates.

Combat

Wars are level-balanced (±10 levels). Attack with Infantry, Naval, Aerial. Engineers build defenses. Medics heal. Spies gather intel. Winner claims hex territories from the loser.

Events & Cards

Real-world events spawn token cards at exact GPS coordinates. Register (50 ) to participate. Each event has a 10-card serie with 1 guaranteed Shiny. If <10 participants you get 1 card guaranteed. If >10, probability + your Chance level determines outcome.

Daily Hunts

One Legendary card released daily. Follow clues, zoom to level 16+ to find it. Chance bonuses and Hunt Hints from the Shop help narrow your search.

Event Rules

Registration

Each event costs 50 to register. Once registered, you wait for the event to close. Your result is revealed as a surprise in the Results tab!

Token Distribution

Each event generates 1 rare hexagonal token tied to the real-world event. If you win, you discover the token and its rarity as a surprise. Your Chance level determines your probability of winning.

Rarity Surprise

When you win, the token's rarity is revealed: Common, Rare, Epic, or Legendary. Higher Chance bonuses from the Shop increase your odds of getting rarer tokens. All tokens are added to your Codex automatically.

Real-World Correlation

Events spawn at exact GPS coordinates of real-world news. Volcanic eruptions, earthquakes, storms, sports events, space launches — each generates unique token cards tied to that moment in history.
